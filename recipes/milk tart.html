Milk tart

Description

In my opinion the milk tart is an ideal treat to make when you are pressed for time .It takes a total of 50 minutes The important thing is to not rush it though. lets begin.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter,softened.
  • 1 cup white sugar.
  • 1 egg.
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • 1 pinch salt.
  • 4 cups milk.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • 1 tablespoon butter.
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch.
  • 1/2 cup white sugar.
  • 2 eggs,beaten.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon.

Step-by-step guide to heaven

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together 1/2 a cup of butter or margarine and 1 cup of sugar add 1 egg and baet until mixture is smooth.Than mix 2 cups of flour, baking powder and salt.Stir the flour mixture into the sugar mixture make sure there thoroughly combined.Press mxture into bottom aand sides of two 9-inch pie pans.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, until golden brown.
  4. In a large saucepan,combine milk,vanilla extract,and 1 tablespoon butter or margarine.Bring to a boil overmedium heatthen remove from burner
  5. In a separate bowl,mix together 2 1/1 tablespoons flour,cornstarch ,and 1/2 cup sugar.Add beaten eggs to sugar mixture and whisk until smooth Slowly whisk mixture into milk.Return pan to heat and bring to a boil,stiring constantly. Boil and stir for 5 minutes.Pour half of the mixture intto each pastry shell. Sprinkle with cinnamon.Chill before serving

Enjoy!
