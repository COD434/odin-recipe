Spaghetti

How to make spaghetti

First lets address the name don't be scared off by the name "spaghetti", its super easy just follow this step by step guide.

Meatballs:

    This is for the meatballs

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 cup fresh bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teapoon ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 egg,beaten

Sauce

  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 5 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2(28 ounce)cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1(6 ounce)can tomato paste
  • 3/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Step-by-step guide

  1. in a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, parsley, parmesan 1/4 teaspoon black pepper,garlic powder and beaten eggs. mix well and form into 12 balls,Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed.
  2. In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.Stir in tomatoes,salt,sugar and bay leaf.Cover,reduce heat to low, and simmer90 minutes.Stir in tomato paste, basil,1/2 teaspoon pepper and meatballs and simmer for 30 minutes more
